Validates that the options used in a section are valid for that section. The valid keys depend on their section and are generated via scraping the documentation.

Consistent with the documentation, this inspection ignores options or sections that start with X-. For more information on the general behaviour see: systemd.unit(5).

Individual section options are described both in their respective man pages (e.g., systemd.service(5)), but additional options are documented in shared sections (e.g., systemd.resource-control(5)).
